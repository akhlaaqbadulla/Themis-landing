Who is Themis?

Themis is a cutting-edge AI legal researcher designed specifically for the Mauritian legal system. By leveraging advanced natural language processing and a comprehensive database of legislations, regulations, and Privy Council judgments, Themis empowers lawyers to retrieve accurate legal advice in seconds.

Named after the Greek Titaness of divine law and order, Themis brings objectivity and precision to legal research, automating the tedious parts of case preparation so professionals can focus on strategy and advocacy.

Our Mission

To democratize access to legal intelligence and streamline the workflow of legal professionals in Mauritius. We believe that technology should serve justice, making it faster, more accessible, and more accurate.
