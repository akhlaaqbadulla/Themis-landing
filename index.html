Frequently Asked Questions

Is Themis trained specifically on Mauritius law?

Yes, exclusively. Themis was built and trained on the complete published corpus of Mauritius legislation: 4,100+ Acts, 57,000+ Supreme Court and Privy Council judgments, 17,800+ regulations, and 150+ bill reprints. It has no knowledge of other jurisdictions by design, which is what makes it so accurate for Mauritian practitioners.

Will Themis use my client data to train its AI?

Never. Your queries, documents, and case notes are never used to train Themis or any underlying AI model. Your data is encrypted, private, and stays yours. Enterprise clients can also request on-premise deployment for full data sovereignty.

How accurate is Themis compared to doing research manually?

Independent testing shows 99.22% accuracy on Mauritius-specific legal queries, and 580% more accurate than generic AI tools like ChatGPT when tested on the same questions. Every output is citation-backed, so you can verify any answer against the source Act or judgment directly.

How long does onboarding take?

Most practitioners are up and running within the same day. Themis requires no installation: it runs in your browser. For enterprise deployments, our team provides dedicated onboarding and training sessions.

Does Themis replace lawyers?

No, and it is not designed to. Themis replaces the research burden, not the lawyer. It handles the time-consuming retrieval, cross-referencing, and drafting work so that you can focus on strategy, client relationships, and advocacy. Think of it as a tireless junior researcher who never sleeps and never makes a citation error.
