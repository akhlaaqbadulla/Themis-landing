Seamless Workflow

1. Legislation Retrieval

Themis scans the entire corpus of Mauritian laws, including the latest amendments. It cross-references Regulations and Acts to ensure the advice is current.

2. Case Law Analysis

It searches relevant judgments from the Supreme Court and the Privy Council. It identifies binding precedents and distinguishes cases based on facts provided.

3. Strategic Advice

Themis synthesizes the information into a coherent memo, outlining the legal position, potential arguments, and recommended course of action.

4. Reporting

Themis provides a detailed report of the legal position, potential arguments, and recommended course of action. all that in your company's document format. With your custom letterheaders, letterfooters, and company logo.

Workflow Integration

Themis integrates seamlessly into your daily tasks.

  • Document Review: Upload contracts or pleadings for instant review.
  • Drafting: Generate legal notices and court documents based on templates.
  • Research: Reduce hours of library research to minutes of AI processing.
